## The Four Effort Levels

| Level | Label | Daily Capacity | Description |
|-------|-------|---------------|-------------|
| E1 | Light | 25% | Low-effort tasks you can sustain indefinitely. Routine maintenance, quick check-ins, light reading. |
| E2 | Moderate | 50% | Meaningful work that requires focus but not peak performance. Writing, planning, steady progress. |
| E3 | Demanding | 75% | High-effort work that taxes your energy significantly. Complex problem-solving, learning new skills, deep creative work. |
| E4 | Maximum | 100% | All-in effort. Peak cognitive demand, high stakes, full immersion. One E4 at a time is the hard ceiling. |

## E-Level Colors (EMRALD Palette)

- E1: `#2D7A4A` (green)
- E2: `#B8912E` (gold)
- E3: `#C06A30` (orange)
- E4: `#B54545` (warm red)
